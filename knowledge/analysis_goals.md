
# Analysis Goals

The agent should help answer questions such as:
- How has revenue changed over time?
- What periods show declining profitability?
- Which metrics are most useful for tracking operating performance?
- What trends or anomalies appear in the financial data?

Retrieved context should support code generation and analysis planning.

---

## Extended Analysis Goals

### 1. Time-Series Analysis
- Analyze long-term trends in revenue, earnings, and market capitalization
- Identify periods of growth, decline, and stability
- Detect seasonality or structural breaks in financial performance

---

### 2. Profitability Analysis
- Examine operating margin trends over time
- Compare revenue growth vs. earnings growth
- Identify periods of declining profitability and potential causes

---

### 3. Valuation Analysis
- Analyze how valuation metrics (P/E, P/S, P/B) change over time
- Identify periods of potential overvaluation or undervaluation
- Compare valuation trends with financial performance

---

### 4. Financial Health and Risk
- Evaluate trends in total debt, total liabilities, and net assets
- Assess leverage and financial risk over time
- Analyze relationships between debt levels and profitability

---

### 5. Shareholder Returns
- Analyze dividend trends and dividend yield over time
- Evaluate consistency of shareholder payouts
- Examine relationship between earnings and dividends

---

### 6. Correlation Analysis
- Identify relationships between key variables:
  - Revenue and earnings
  - Earnings and EPS
  - Debt and liabilities
- Use correlation matrices to detect strong dependencies

---

### 7. Predictive Analysis
- Build models to predict:
  - Earnings from revenue and margins
  - Market capitalization from financial indicators
- Evaluate model performance and interpret results

---

### 8. Anomaly Detection
- Identify outliers or unusual financial patterns
- Investigate potential causes (economic shocks, accounting changes)
- Assess impact of anomalies on analysis results

---

### 9. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Use visualizations such as:
  - Line plots for time trends
  - Histograms for distributions
  - Boxplots for variability
- Identify patterns, distributions, and irregularities

---

## Conclusion

These analysis goals aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of McDonald’s financial performance, valuation, and risk profile over time. The agent should support both exploratory and advanced analytical tasks, enabling deeper insights into trends and relationships within the dataset.
